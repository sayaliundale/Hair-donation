Why People Donate Hairs?

Donating hair can help people who have lost their hair due to medical conditions such as cancer, alopecia, or burns. By making wigs from donated hair, individuals can contribute to improving the quality of life for those who have lost their hair.

Donating hair can also be a way to show support for friends or family members who are going through hair loss due to a medical condition.

Donating hair can be a way to make a positive impact on the world by giving back to the community and helping those in need

Some people choose to donate their hair for personal reasons such as a desire for a change in hairstyle or as a way to honor a loved one who has battled a medical condition.
